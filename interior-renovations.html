Our Interior Renovation Process

1

Design Consultation

We start with a comprehensive consultation to understand your vision, lifestyle needs, and budget. Our team assesses your space and discusses design options, materials, and package choices that best fit your goals.

2

Planning & Permits

We create detailed plans and handle all necessary permits and approvals. Our team coordinates with suppliers, schedules tradespeople, and ensures everything is ready for a smooth project start.

3

Preparation & Demolition

We protect your home and belongings, then carefully remove existing fixtures and finishes. All debris is properly disposed of and the space is prepared for new construction.

4

Construction & Installation

Our skilled team handles all construction work including electrical, plumbing, framing, and finishing. We maintain clean work areas and provide regular progress updates throughout the project.

5

Final Inspection & Cleanup

We complete a thorough final inspection, address any touch-ups, and perform a complete cleanup. You'll receive warranty information and our commitment to ongoing support.
