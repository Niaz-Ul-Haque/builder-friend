About Plan6ix Buildtrade Inc

We're a Toronto-based renovation and construction company committed to transforming your vision into reality. Since 2020, we've been helping homeowners and businesses create beautiful, functional spaces with quality craftsmanship and reliable service.

Our Story

Founded on Quality and Trust

Plan6ix Buildtrade Inc was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to provide homeowners and businesses in the Greater Toronto Area with reliable, high-quality renovation services at fair prices. What started as a small team of experienced craftsmen has grown into one of the most trusted renovation companies in the GTA.

Commitment to Excellence

From our first project to our most recent completion, we've maintained an unwavering commitment to quality craftsmanship, transparent communication, and customer satisfaction. Every member of our team shares this commitment, and it shows in every project we complete.

Growing with Toronto

As Toronto continues to grow and evolve, so do we. We've expanded our services, refined our processes, and built lasting relationships with suppliers and subcontractors who share our commitment to quality. Today, we're proud to serve communities throughout the GTA with comprehensive renovation services.

How We Work

Our proven process ensures every project is completed on time, on budget, and to your complete satisfaction. Here's how we turn your vision into reality.

1

Free Consultation

We start with a comprehensive consultation to understand your vision, needs, and budget. Our team visits your property to assess the space and discuss options. This consultation is completely free with no obligations.

2

Design & Planning

We create detailed plans and help you choose materials, finishes, and package options. Our team handles all permits and coordinates with suppliers to ensure everything is ready for a smooth start.

3

Transparent Proposal

You'll receive a detailed proposal with clear pricing, timeline, and scope of work. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden fees or surprise charges throughout your project.

4

Professional Execution

Our skilled team handles every aspect of your project with quality craftsmanship and attention to detail. We maintain clean work areas and provide regular progress updates.

5

Quality Completion

We complete your project with thorough cleanup and final walkthrough. You'll receive warranty information and our commitment to ongoing support and service.
