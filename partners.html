CanSave - Building Materials Excellence

40+ Years in Business Canadian Owned & Operated

CanSave has been a cornerstone of the Canadian building materials industry for over four decades. As a trusted distributor of high-quality building materials and manufacturer of Cabinetsmith and Doorsmith products, they provide the foundation for our exceptional renovation projects.

What CanSave Brings to Our Projects:

  • Premium Building Materials: Comprehensive selection of construction and renovation materials
  • Cabinetsmith Products: High-quality custom cabinetry solutions for kitchens and bathrooms
  • Doorsmith Manufacturing: Professional-grade doors and hardware for interior and exterior applications
  • Canadian Quality: Proudly Canadian-owned with a commitment to local craftsmanship
  • Reliable Supply Chain: Consistent availability and timely delivery for project schedules

How This Partnership Benefits You:

Our relationship with CanSave ensures access to premium building materials at competitive prices, supporting our ability to deliver exceptional value across all our Budget, Standard, and Modern package options. Their four decades of industry expertise complement our project management excellence.

Factory Direct Lighting - Canadian LED Innovation

Canadian LED Manufacturer DLC & ETL Certified Energy Star Approved

Factory Direct Lighting (FDL) designs and manufactures premier LED lighting solutions right here in Canada. Their commitment to efficiency, elegance, and affordability makes them the perfect partner for our residential and commercial lighting needs.

FDL's Comprehensive Lighting Solutions:

  • Residential Lighting: Recessed downlights, flush mount fixtures, under-cabinet lighting, and smart ceiling lights
  • Commercial Solutions: High bay fixtures, panel lights, linear strips, and emergency lighting systems
  • Outdoor Applications: Flood lights, area lighting, wall packs, and parking lot illumination
  • Specialty Products: Fire-rated series, vapor-tight fixtures, and trimless gimbal systems
  • Smart Technology: RGB smart ceiling lights with tunable white and wireless control options
  • Energy Efficiency: All products designed for maximum energy savings and longevity
