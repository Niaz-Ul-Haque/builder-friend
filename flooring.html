Our Professional Installation Process

1

Consultation & Material Selection

We assess your space, discuss your needs and lifestyle, and help you choose the perfect flooring material. We'll provide samples and discuss the best options for your specific application and budget.

2

Measurement & Estimation

Our team provides precise measurements and detailed estimates including all materials, labor, and additional services like old flooring removal and subfloor preparation.

3

Preparation & Removal

We protect your home, remove existing flooring if needed, and prepare the subfloor. This includes leveling, cleaning, and any necessary repairs to ensure a perfect installation foundation.

4

Underlayment Installation

We install appropriate underlayment or padding based on your flooring type. This crucial step ensures comfort, sound reduction, and longevity of your new flooring.

5

Professional Installation

Our skilled installers precisely install your flooring using proper techniques and tools. We ensure proper expansion gaps, seamless transitions, and beautiful finished edges.

6

Finishing & Cleanup

We install baseboards and trim, perform final inspections, and complete thorough cleanup. You'll receive care instructions and warranty information for your new flooring.
