Our Professional Hardscaping Process

1

Site Assessment & Design

We evaluate your property's drainage, soil conditions, and existing landscape. Our team creates detailed design plans that optimize functionality, aesthetics, and integration with your home's architecture.

2

Permits & Planning

We handle all necessary permits and coordinate with utility location services. Our detailed planning includes material calculations, timeline development, and coordination with any additional trades.

3

Excavation & Site Preparation

Professional excavation to proper depths and grades, followed by soil compaction and base preparation. We install proper drainage systems and ensure stable foundations for long-lasting results.

4

Base Installation & Grading

We install engineered base materials with proper compaction and grading for optimal drainage and stability. This critical step ensures your hardscaping will perform well for decades.

5

Stone Installation & Finishing

Precise installation of your chosen materials using professional techniques and tools. We ensure proper joint spacing, level surfaces, and beautiful pattern alignment throughout your project.

6

Sealing & Final Details

We complete joint sealing, edge restraints, and final compaction. After thorough cleanup, we provide maintenance instructions and warranty information for your investment protection.
